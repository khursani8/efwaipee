About Us

Welcome to Centre for Graduate Studies at Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS. Our mission is to guide and support students throughout their university experience from admission to graduation. If you are a student, our goal is simple: to promote your success. Through the Office of Admissions, we hope to facilitate your smooth entry into the University. Through the One-Stop Shop and the Office of all Aids, we seek to get you registered for the course that you need and assist you in applying for financial aid / sponsorship. The Graduation Office helps to facilitate your transition from a student to a degree-holding graduate and researcher while you are at Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS, and in the years beyond graduation
